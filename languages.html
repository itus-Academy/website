Objectives

Foreign languages provide a competitive edge in career choices: one is able to communicate in a second language. Foreign language study enhances listening skills and memory. One participates more effectively and responsibly in a multi-cultural world if one knows another language.

At Itus Academy, of the incentives given to enrolled children, French language is set up for parents who wants their children to learn foreign language. It helps their communication skills and gives them better opportunities and can make up their Curriculum Vitae to apply for opportunities around the world.
